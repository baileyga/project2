Yoga

Yoga has its roots from Hinduim and Buddhist practices, but it is practiced by those outside of those cultures for mental and physical health. You can also attend yoga classes if you are interested in practicing yoga but are unsure where to start.

There are different variations of yoga and these variations influence the types of positions that are utilized. Some types of yoga are Kundalini yoga, Restorative yoga, and Vinyasa yoga.
